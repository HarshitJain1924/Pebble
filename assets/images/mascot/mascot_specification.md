# Mascot Visual Specification: Pebble the Raven

This document locks the character design guidelines for Pebble the Raven to ensure high visual consistency across all expression states.

---

## 1. Character Identity & Personality
- **Name**: Pebble
- **Species**: Raven
- **Age Archetype**: Young adult
- **Vibe**: Curious, smart, supportive, and slightly mischievous.
- **Design Philosophy**: Character-first, bird-second. Pebble is a friendly productivity companion, not a realistic wildlife illustration.

---

## 2. Proportions & Shape Language
- **Head**: Large, rounded, and clean head shape (similar to the Duolingo Owl's clean geometry).
- **Beak**: Slightly oversized, sharp but friendly dark-gray/metallic beak.
- **Eyes**: Large, highly expressive round eyes with distinct pupils and eyebrows that convey direct emotion (no lifeless black dot eyes).
- **Body & Wing**: Slim silhouette. Only head, shoulder, and a partial wing should be visible.
- **Outline Style**: Clean, modern, high-contrast flat vector outline suitable for premium SaaS interfaces.

---

## 3. Color Palette
- **Primary Body**: Deep blue-black (`#181825` to `#1E1E2E`)
- **Accents / Highlights**: Slate gray / dark indigo (`#313244` / `#45475A`)
- **Beak**: Metallic dark charcoal / gray (`#11111B` / `#585B70`)
- **Eyes**: Bright white/light-blue (`#CDD6F4` / `#F5E0DC`)

---

## 4. Emotional State Changes
To maintain consistency, all states must use the exact same bird model, head shape, and color scheme, changing **only**:
- **Idle**: Default smart expression, neutral eyelids.
- **Curious**: Head tilted, eyes looking slightly inward/downward toward screen content.
- **Happy**: Friendly squinting eyes, soft smile lines around the beak.
- **Worried**: Eyes wider, eyebrows angled downward in concern (streak risk).
- **Celebrating**: Eyes closed with happy arcs, sparkle effects around head.
- **Focused**: Small round wire-rimmed glasses or intense, focused eyebrows.
- **Sleeping**: Eyes curved closed, peaceful breathing expression.
